== Description ==

The Online Outbox plugin allows you to easily add a subscription form to your WordPress site, to grow your list of email subscribers of your Email Newsletters, Mass Email Marketing messages, Email Autoresponders, Email Surveys, Optin Emails, Email Marketing Statistics.

You can customize the form and the mail contents flexibly with simple markup. The form supports Ajax-powered submitting, CAPTCHA, Akismet spam filtering and so on.

Requires an account at www.OnlineOutbox.com. Accounts sending fewer than 500 emails per month are free.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the entire `online-outbox-subscription-form` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.

You will find 'Online Outbox' menu in your WordPress admin panel.

For basic usage, you can also have a look at the [plugin homepage](http://www.onlineoutbox.com/plugins/wordpress/).
